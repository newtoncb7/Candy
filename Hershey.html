Hershey's Chocolate

There's a reason why certain things in life earn the label "classic". Hershey's chocolate makes it on this list because of the nostalgia I feel everytime I take a bite. I included Hershey's Kisses as well. Although the chocolate is the same, I am personally convinced it has a unique taste and experience. How can you not love them?!
